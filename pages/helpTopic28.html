A Better Finder Rename 11 Help
    Creating Droplets
 

  1. Launch A Better Finder Rename by double clicking it
  2. Select the action you want to automate and set its parameters appropriately.
  3. Now instead of clicking "Perform Renames" to rename the files simply click on the "Create Droplet..." button.
A file selection dialog will appear.
  1. Provide a meaningful name for your droplet application and click "Save"
